Target Audience

The target audience for this website is going to be anyone you wants to know what the weather is going to be. It will also target people looking to plan outdoor activities.

Personas

  • Name: Billy
  • Age: 28
  • Visit Purpose: Billy has family in town and wants to plan some activites they can do based on the weather.
  • Name: Abi
  • Age: 18
  • Visit Purpose: Abi is wanting to take her family to the beach this weekend. She gets on Rain or Shain to look at what the weather is going to be like.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: Taking a all day fishing trip? Check the weather so you dress right
  • Scenario 2: Out Kayacking when it starts raining? Check our mobile app to see when it's going to clear up.
  • Scenario 3: Planning on going hiking, but the weather isn't going to be good? Check out website for good? altentive activities
  • Scenario 4: Have family coming into town? Hop onto our website and look for family friendly outdoor activities
  • Scenario 5: Check out website to know what to pack when you want to go camping.
